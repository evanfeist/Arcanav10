Rules & Scoring

Goal

Be first to reach 60 points.

Turn

On your turn, do exactly one: Construct, Action (6/7/9), Sigil (J/Q/K), or Discard. Then draw 1.

Constructs

Pair=2 pts; True Pair (same suit)=5 pts.
Set (3+ of a kind): 3=2×rank+2; 4=3×rank (or 4×rank if all four suits); 5+=3×rank+2.
Sets with jokers: 4→3×rank, 5+→3×rank+2; no suit bonus unless size ≥6. −1 pt per Joker.

Runs

Same suit, 3+ in sequence (jokers can fill gaps). Score = sum of ranks +1 per card. −1 per Joker. No suit bonus if any Joker used.

Joker-only

2J=4 pts; 3J=7 pts + draw 3; 4J=15 pts + refill to 7. (Ignore Joker cost/nerf.)

Suit Bonuses (no Jokers unless noted)

♥: +2 pts · ♦: d6 → steal 1/2/3 pts · ♠: steal up to 2 random cards · ♣: extra turn (max chain 2).
Sets ≥6 cards can still get suit bonus even with Jokers.

Actions & Ace

6: Draw 2. · 7: Steal random card from opponent. · 9: Swap (give 1, take 1 random).
Ace may block 7/9 (house rules apply in engine).
